The Problem

A $140B counterfeiting crisis with no scalable answer

The luxury resale market is exploding — and so is the fraud. Existing authentication is slow, expensive, subjective, and human-dependent. There is no infrastructure built for scale.

  • ✕
    Manual expert appraisals cost $300–$2,000 per item and take days or weeks — unworkable at marketplace velocity.
  • ✕
    Grey market diversion strips brands of pricing power and floods resale channels with untraceable inventory.
  • ✕
    Buyers bear 100% of counterfeit risk with zero recourse — destroying trust in the entire secondary market ecosystem.
  • ✕
    Provenance data is siloed, paper-based, and easily forged. No single source of truth exists for ownership history.
